Click empty part of graph to get focus, enable left or right arrow key scrolling through chart

Click on a circle on line in graph. This will hightlight all points from that reading red and specifies an alert.

The data is raw, un-normalized; indicate at least three or more alerts. Each line represents a different vital sign for a patient. Systolic, Diastolic, HR1, Ox, HR2, Weight. There is no total alert limit, but at least three.

There is no unclick,undo feature. Just reload the page(Ctrl-R), and I will use the results from the last logged results per patient. There are 10 files.
